## 🚀 About Us

Team Birbal Sahni is a multidisciplinary robotics team participating in the AI + Robotics Bootcamp 2026. Our members bring together expertise in software engineering, artificial intelligence, embedded systems, electronics, and mechanical design.

By combining creativity, engineering principles, and collaborative problem-solving, we aim to develop impactful robotic systems while strengthening our technical and leadership skills.

---

## 🦾 Robotic Arm Development

Our primary focus is the design and development of a multi-axis robotic arm capable of precise motion control and future intelligent automation capabilities.

### Key Features

* Multi-axis articulated movement
* Precision servo motor control
* ESP32-based control architecture
* PCA9685 servo driver integration
* Modular mechanical structure
* Real-time control system
* Expandable for AI-based applications
